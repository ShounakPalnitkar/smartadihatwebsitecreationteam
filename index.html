Advanced Wearable Computing Platform

Precision Engineering

The SmartVision Pro features a Raspberry Pi 5 computing core (2.4GHz Cortex-A76) managing six ultrasonic HC-SR04 sensors with temperature compensation for 360° obstacle detection (92.2% accuracy). Our dual-camera system combines a Sony IMX500 (8MP daylight vision) with Raspberry Pi's IR-enabled IMX219 module, ensuring 24/7 environmental awareness.

The ergonomic design incorporates active thermal management (heatsink + fan) maintaining stable operation below 93°C. A smart power system dynamically allocates energy from our 10,000mAh battery (2.5-3 hour runtime), balancing between compute-intensive vision processing and essential proximity sensing.
